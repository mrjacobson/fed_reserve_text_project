 Meeting of the Federal Open Market CommitteeDecember 12, 1980 Minutes of Actions A meeting of the Federal Open Market Committee was held on Friday, December 12, 1980, at 12:40 p.m., at the call of Chairman Volcker. This was a telephone conference meeting, and each individual was in Washington, D.
