 Meeting of Federal Open Market CommitteeMarch 2, 1979 MINUTES OF ACTIONSA meeting of the Federal Open Market Committee was held on Friday, March 2, 1979, at 12:30 p.m., at the call of Chairman Miller. This was a telephone conference meeting, and each individual was in Washington, D.
