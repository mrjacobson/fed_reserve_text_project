 Meeting of Federal Open Market CommitteeMarch 7, 1980 MINUTES OF ACTIONS A meeting of the Federal Open Market Committee was held on Friday, March 7, 1980. This was a telephone conference meeting, and each individual was in Washington, D. C., except as otherwise indicated in parentheses in the following list of those participating.
