 Meeting of the Federal Open Market CommitteeFebruary 24, 1981 Minutes of Actions A meeting of the Federal Open Market Committee was held on Tuesday, February 24, 1981, at 12:00 noon, at the call of Chairman Volcker. This was a telephone conference meeting, and each individual was in Washington, D.
